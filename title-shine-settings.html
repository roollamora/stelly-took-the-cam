Key Issues & Solutions:

  • Clipping Issue: background-clip: text can clip tall letters/glyphs
  • Alignment: Pseudo-element needs exact same font properties
  • Height: calc(100% + 0.2em) makes pseudo-element taller to prevent clipping
  • Padding: 0.1em top padding gives text breathing room
  • Position: -0.1em top offset compensates for extra height
